A cappuccino, a flat white, a matcha latte. Whatever you steam your milk for, dairy or oat, it always starts with the pitcher. So we made one worth reaching for, and keeping on show.

The ritual

The best moment of the day deserves the best tool.

Philosophy

Understated, by design.

Nothing decorative, nothing loud. Honest materials, a heavier-than-standard steel body, and matte finishes chosen with restraint. Made to be used every day, for years.

In every detail

For every day. For years.

We wanted a pitcher that looks as good as it pours. Matte, quiet, better made than the rest.

Heavier gauge

A ~0.9 mm wall, against the usual ~0.6 mm. More mass for steadier steaming and a solid, balanced feel.

Seamless handle

No welds to crack, no seam for milk to catch or burn onto. Just a cleaner line.

Matte finish

A soft, understated matte finish that hides everyday marks. Hand-washing keeps the colour at its best.

Precision spout

Sharply drawn for clean pours and latte art. Cappuccinos and flat whites, dialled in.

18/10 stainless

A food-grade stainless body, built for years of daily use.
