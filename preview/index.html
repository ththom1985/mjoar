Our story

Good coffee deserves good tools. We made the ones we couldn't find.

Nothing decorative, nothing loud. Honest materials, a heavier-than-standard steel body, and matte finishes chosen with restraint. Made to be used every day, for years.

The craft

Made to be used. Made to last.

We make the tools we wanted on our own bars: precise, matte, and built better.

  • BodyFood-grade 18/10 stainless steel
  • Heavier gauge~0.9 mm wall vs. the usual ~0.6 mm — more thermal mass for steadier steaming and a solid, balanced feel
  • Seamless handleNo weld points — cleaner lines, and more hygienic
  • FinishHard-wearing matte coating, scratch- and dishwasher-resistant
  • SpoutSharply drawn for controlled latte art
  • Sizes350 & 600 ml

And underneath, a small engraved smile — a private detail, just for you.
